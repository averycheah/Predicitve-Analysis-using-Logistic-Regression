# Predicitve-Analysis-using-Logistic-Regression
# Digital Payment Adoption Among Malaysian SMEs

- Analyzes factors influencing digital payment adoption among Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) in Malaysia
- Dataset collected from SME owners via Google Forms survey
- Uses logistic regression to predict full digital payment adoption within the next 6–12 months

## Key Variables
- Business size
- Industry type
- Duration of operation
- Perceptions and attitudes toward digital payments

## Key Findings
- Many SMEs show strong intention to adopt digital payment methods
- Significant predictors include:
  - Ease of management
  - Perceived affordability
  - Customer demand
- Model performance:
  - 77% prediction accuracy
  - AUC score of 0.86

## Implications
- Government incentives and reduced transaction fees can increase adoption
- Technical support is critical for SMEs
- Ongoing concerns include security and regulatory clarity
- Findings support policymakers and stakeholders in strengthening Malaysia’s digital economy
